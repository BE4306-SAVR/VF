Overview of the SAVR Module:
This module will provide students with a comprehensive understanding of Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR) technologies and their applications, as well as real-time rendering techniques for interactive and immersive experiences.
Students will learn about the hardware and software required for AR/VR and will explore various software and technology to prepare the contents.
Throughout the module, students will be challenged to create interactive AR/VR prototypes, learning the fundamentals of 3D modeling, animation, and game engine development. They will also explore real-time rendering pipelines, including lighting, shading, and effects, as well as optimization techniques for high-performance graphics on various hardware platforms.
The module will culminate with projects that integrates all the skills learned, where students will apply their knowledge to design and develop a real-time Interactive AR/VR environment.
The end goal of the module is to provide students with the technical knowledge and practical experience necessary to enter into the growing AR/VR industry. 

Diploma in Interior Design program:
Our studio-based learning encourages critical thinking and creativity under the guidance of experienced mentors. We combine traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge digital spatial experimentation, introducing design thinking, user experience and the latest technologies. 
Marking skills empower you to create and effectively promote remarkable designs. Be at the forefront of the ever-evolving built and environment industry, from 3D modelling to sustainability practices. 
We emphasize sustainability by teaching eco-friendly materials, energy-efficient lightings, and responsible design. Singapore's construction landscape is growing, increasing the demand for skilled interior designers who play a significant role in shaping the city's architectural identity.

Talk to us!

We believe that innovation and progress are fueled by collaboration and knowledge sharing. That's why we're committed to being open and actively seeking industry collaboration in the field of Virtual Reality (VR) and architectural design.

We recognize that the future of design and architecture is a collective responsibility, and that together, we can achieve far more than we can alone. By partnering with industry leaders, startups, and research institutions, we aim to:

- Advance the state-of-the-art in VR technology and its applications in design
- Share knowledge, resources, and expertise to drive innovation
- Co-create solutions that address real-world challenges and improve the built environment
- Foster a community that promotes open dialogue, idea exchange, and mutual growth

Our doors are open to collaboration in various forms, including:

- Research partnerships to explore new VR applications and design methods
- Co-development of new tools and features to enhance design workflows
- Joint projects to create showcase VR experiences that demonstrate the potential of immersive design
- Knowledge sharing through workshops, webinars, and conferences

By working together, we can unlock the full potential of VR in architecture and design, and create a future where immersive experiences empower designers, architects, and users alike to create better, more sustainable, and more inspiring spaces for everyone.

Let's collaborate and shape the future of design together!

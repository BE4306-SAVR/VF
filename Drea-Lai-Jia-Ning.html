CONCEPT STATEMENT:
Harmony Expo VR Showcase: Nurturing the Mind, Body, and Soul.
Creating a revolutionary trade show venue that redefines the skincare purchasing experience.
Harmony Expo VR Showcase stands out by providing a flexible platform capable of hosting a variety of events. Whether it is for a product launch, trade show, internal or external company showcase, the dynamic VR environment can be adapted seamlessly to provide a customized experience for every occasion.
Interactivity takes center stage, offering users the opportunity to actively engage in a virtual space that mirrors the richness of physical interactions. From dynamic interactions with exhibitors to participating in group workshops and networking, the Harmony Expo VR Showcase provides a holistic experience for the body and the mind.
Elevating beyond a mere display of skincare products, the Harmony Expo VR Showcase provides users an immersive wellness experience. The meticulously designed ambience is crafted to evoke a profound sense of tranquility and rejuvenation, transforming the virtual encounter into a holistic and immersive journey for users.
Embark on a virtual journey with the groundbreaking concept presented by the Harmony Expo VR Showcase where the cutting-edge skincare solutions meet mindful well-being. The Harmony Expo VR Showcase redefines the possibilities of future trade shows where adaptability, interactivity, and flexibility converge.

Drea Lai Jia Ning

dljn@gmail.com

Bus Shelter Project

click on the image to experience the Panoramic VR

CONCEPTUAL STATEMENT:
Intelligent Oasis: Incorporating smart technology to create opportunities for disruptive innovations to enhance users' engagements.
• Live bus scheduling updates on interactive digital touchscreens strategically placed at both ends of the bus stop for comprehensive visibility
• Smart applications showcasing latest news, GPS, maps and travel information (eg nearby attractions & eateries) and interactive gaming experiences.
• Integrate sensors to monitor and display real-time climate conditions within the shelter.
• Targeted advertising opportunities
Connected Oasis: Utilizing user-centric technologies to improve efficiency and convenience of commuting by public transport
• Wifi-enabled to allow users access to real-time information and news.
• Smart charging ports for charging mobile and digital devices.
Green Oasis: Integrating sustainable features with eco-design to improve comfort and aesthetic appeal of the environment.
• Achieving complete energy independence with integration of an expansive green roof to mitigate external heat by harnessing solar power through
strategically installed solar panels
• Solar panels power USB ports and lighting, ensuring sustainable energy for bus stop amenities day and night.
• Louvers integrated along the bus stop optimize airflow.
Cozy & Accessible Oasis: Creating a welcoming and enjoyable environment for users, fostering a sense of well-being while waiting for public transportation.
• A mixture of aluminum and hardwood which are durable and weather-resistant materials for the seats to ensure longevity and ease of maintenance and optimal comfort for commuters of all ages.
• Inclusive design with priority seating and tactile paths to improve accessibility.
• Extended overhangs to provide additional coverage during rain.
• Anti-slip flooring to prevent slipping accidents.

CONCEPT STATEMENT:
CHOSEN EVENT: News Telecast
The innovative concept behind my virtual studio design revolves around the interplay of height, color, and shapes to curate a captivating and immersive ambiance. This redefines the traditional studio setting, offering a visual and sensory experience for both the audience and us.
Height is leveraged to introduce multi-dimensional dynamism. The elevations create distinct zones, each with its own purpose and character. This design not only facilitates efficient spatial use but also shows a sense of hierarchy, emphasising the focal points and enhancing visual interest. Elevated platforms house diverse elements, from the anchor desk to the screen displays where advertisement or sponsorships could be added on, engaging the audience on various levels.
Neon lights are bright and selected to stimulate creativity, and establish a cohesive brand identity. Chromatic transitions sweep across the studio, guiding viewers' attention and shaping their mood. Warm hues infuse energy, while the cooler tones help to induce focus, allowing the studio to adapt its ambiance to different content genres and narratives.
The integration of shapes further enriches the environment. Organic curves soften the overall aesthetic, creating an inviting atmosphere, while angular structures inject a modern and edgy vibe. These shapes are not only visually interesting but also adds on to the visual hierarchy on where your eyes travel first.
By also adding lighting such as the ones along the stage platforms, not only does it add interesting colours to the stage but also help illuminate and bring focus to the area, pulling the eye to the stage.

Bus Shelter Project

Nur Farah Binti Juffri

farahjuffri.21@ichat.sp.edu.sg

Bus Shelter Project

click on the image to experience the Panoramic VR

CONCEPTUAL STATEMENT:
To create a comprehensive bus stop design that creatively addresses subjects of concern that not only serves a functional intent but also blends seamlessly into the environment visually and aesthetically. Digitalization of signboard through solar power shall aid in continuously improving this generation.
With the increasingly worrying climate change, the use of sustainable materials aim to reduce negative impact on environment as well as create greener conditions. It will reduce carbon monoxide released into the air as well as lower greenhouse gases.
Choice of material selection such as wood is one of the most sustainable & environmentally friendly construction material due to it's absorption of carbon dioxide while growing.
Considerations are taken in such as adaptability of design and whether it can be recycled if at the possibility of it being taken in the future.

Introduction


Welcome to the immersive world of Singapore Polytechnic's School of Architecture and the Built Environment! 
Our website is a dynamic showcase, featuring some of our outstanding student works in the realm of Interactive Virtual Reality (VR), Panoramic VR and Animation. Here, innovation converges with creativity as our students push the boundaries of architectural and built environment exploration through the lens of virtual experiences.

From groundbreaking designs to interactive urban simulations, each project represents a journey into the future of spatial understanding. As you navigate through these virtual landscapes, crafted by the next generation of architectural visionaries, you'll witness the fusion of cutting-edge technology and design prowess. Our students, driven by a passion for immersive storytelling, bring to life captivating narratives that redefine how we interact with and perceive our surroundings.

Join us on this virtual odyssey through the School of Architecture and the Built Environment at Singapore Polytechnic. Experience the intersection of imagination and innovation as we proudly present the students' extraordinary works in Interactive Virtual Reality. Welcome to a world where ideas flourish, designs transcend, and the future of architecture unfolds in the digital realm.

Project Showcase


Above is our showcase of innovative design projects from the students taking the module BE4306 | Space in Augmented and Virtual Reality (SAVR) and the Final Year Projects (FYP) from the Diploma in Interior Design (DID). Our program is dedicated to equipping students with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in the ever-evolving fields of design and technology.

At the heart of our mission is a commitment to fostering creativity, collaboration, and critical thinking among our students. Through hands-on projects like Bus Shelter Design, Virtual Studio Set Design, and Civic Interior Design, our students explore the intersection of art, technology, and functionality. These projects not only highlight their technical skills but also their ability to address real-world challenges and create impactful solutions.

This design aims to create an engaging and user-friendly platform for showcasing student work, highlighting the innovative and creative applications of VR and spatial design.
